Economics

I love economics because it has challenged my intuition and shifted my worldview. It is pervasive in all areas of study – and I love its applicability.

HPEC 2016

Harvard’s Pre-Collegiate Economics Challenge

I was extremely fortunate to be able to represent both my school and my country at this renowned economics competition. I was able to network with like-minded students from around the world, delve deeper into my passion, and meet my long-time inspiration Professor Gregory Mankiw. My team of four posted the highest score in the written portion of the competition, and qualified as one of the top 16 teams; this is just after learning one year of economics!
